# How to Send Newsletter Emails

## Quick Method (Copy & Paste into Email)

### Step 1: Get All Email Addresses
1. Log into your **Admin Dashboard**
2. Scroll down to **"Newsletter Subscribers"** section
3. Click the **"Copy All Emails"** button (purple button)
4. You'll see a confirmation showing how many emails were copied

### Step 2: Send Email to All Subscribers

#### Option A: Using Gmail
1. Open Gmail and click **"Compose"**
2. Click **"BCC"** (Blind Carbon Copy) at the top right
3. **Paste** the emails into the BCC field (Ctrl+V or Cmd+V)
4. Add your email in the "To" field
5. Write your newsletter subject and content
6. Click **"Send"**

**Why BCC?** Protects subscriber privacy - they won't see each other's emails.

#### Option B: Using Outlook
1. Open Outlook and click **"New Email"**
2. Click **"BCC"** button in the ribbon
3. **Paste** the emails into the BCC field
4. Add your email in the "To" field
5. Write your newsletter
6. Click **"Send"**

#### Option C: Using Apple Mail
1. Open Mail and click **"New Message"**
2. In the header, click the dropdown next to "To:"
3. Select **"BCC"**
4. **Paste** the emails into the BCC field
5. Add your email in the "To" field
6. Write your newsletter
7. Click **"Send"**

## Professional Method (Using Email Marketing Service)

### Why Use Email Marketing Services?
- Track who opens emails
- Track who clicks links
- Beautiful templates
- Automated campaigns
- Avoid spam filters
- Schedule emails in advance

### Recommended Services

#### 1. Mailchimp (Free for up to 500 subscribers)
**Steps:**
1. Go to [mailchimp.com](https://mailchimp.com) and create account
2. Click **"Audience"** ‚Üí **"Import Contacts"**
3. In your admin dashboard, click **"Export CSV"**
4. Upload the CSV file to Mailchimp
5. Create a campaign and send!

#### 2. SendGrid (Free for 100 emails/day)
**Steps:**
1. Go to [sendgrid.com](https://sendgrid.com) and sign up
2. Go to **"Marketing"** ‚Üí **"Contacts"**
3. Click **"Import Contacts"**
4. Upload your CSV from admin dashboard
5. Create and send campaign

#### 3. Constant Contact (Free trial)
**Steps:**
1. Go to [constantcontact.com](https://constantcontact.com)
2. Click **"Contacts"** ‚Üí **"Add Contacts"**
3. Upload CSV from your admin dashboard
4. Design email with drag-and-drop editor
5. Send newsletter

## Export Options Available

### 1. Copy All Emails (Quick Send)
- **Button:** Purple "Copy All Emails" button
- **Format:** `email1@example.com, email2@example.com, email3@example.com`
- **Best For:** Sending one-time emails through Gmail/Outlook
- **Action:** Automatically copies to clipboard

### 2. Export CSV (Professional)
- **Button:** Green "Export CSV" button
- **Format:** Spreadsheet with Email, Date, Status columns
- **Best For:** Importing to Mailchimp, SendGrid, Constant Contact
- **File Name:** `newsletter-subscribers-2025-12-19.csv`

## Sending Schedule Recommendations

### Frequency
- **Start with:** Once per week (every Friday)
- **Special events:** Extra emails for holidays
- **Maximum:** 2-3 emails per week (don't annoy people)

### Best Times to Send
- **Weekdays:** Tuesday-Thursday, 10 AM - 12 PM
- **Weekends:** Saturday 9 AM or Sunday 5 PM
- **Avoid:** Monday mornings (inbox overload)

### What to Send
- **Weekly:** Best deals of the week
- **Bi-weekly:** New product arrivals
- **Monthly:** Long-form content, trends, recipes
- **Special:** Holidays, events, flash sales

## Tracking Your Success

### Metrics to Watch
1. **Open Rate**: How many people opened email
   - Good: 15-25%
   - Great: 25%+

2. **Click Rate**: How many clicked links
   - Good: 2-5%
   - Great: 5%+

3. **Unsubscribe Rate**: How many unsubscribed
   - Good: Less than 0.5%
   - Warning: More than 2% (sending too often)

### Improve Results
- Test different subject lines
- Send at different times
- Try different content formats
- Ask subscribers what they want
