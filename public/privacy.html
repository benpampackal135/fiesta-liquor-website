Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

Fiesta Liquor ("we", "us", "our") operates this website and is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our website:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number.
  • Order Information: Products ordered, order history, delivery/pickup details.
  • Payment Information: Payment card details are processed securely by Stripe. We do not store full card numbers on our servers.
  • Location Information: Delivery address and ZIP code to verify delivery eligibility.
  • Account Information: Login credentials (email and hashed password) if you create an account.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information, and usage data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders (pickup and delivery).
  • Verify your age and eligibility to purchase alcohol.
  • Send order confirmations, receipts, and status updates.
  • Respond to your questions and provide customer support.
  • Improve our website, products, and services.
  • Send marketing emails or newsletters (only if you opt in).

3. How Your Information Is Stored and Secured

  • Payments are processed by Stripe, a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor. Card numbers and CVV are handled directly by Stripe and are not stored on our servers.
  • User accounts and orders are stored securely on our backend hosted by Railway.
  • Authentication may be handled using Firebase Authentication for secure login.

4. Who We Share Your Information With

We share your information only with trusted service providers necessary to operate our business:

  • Stripe: For secure payment processing.
  • Twilio or SMS providers: To send order and delivery text notifications.
  • Email providers: To send order confirmations and updates.
  • Delivery partners (if used): To deliver your order to the correct address.

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to:

  • Comply with legal and tax obligations.
  • Maintain accurate order and accounting records.
  • Provide customer support and resolve disputes.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You may:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we have about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link.

To make a privacy request, contact us at: info@fiestaliquor.com.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep items in your cart.
  • Remember that you confirmed you are 21+.
  • Improve site performance and user experience.

8. Children and Age Restrictions

This website is intended only for individuals 21 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 21.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
